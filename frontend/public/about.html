About TST PlotConnect

TST PlotConnect is a verified listings platform built to make land and rental discovery faster, safer, and more transparent across Kenya. We bring together vetted plots, clear locations, and direct contact access so buyers and renters can make confident decisions.

From Machakos to Nairobi and growing counties, you can filter by area, compare listings, and view locations on the map. Our mission is to reduce fraud, save time, and connect people to trusted property opportunities.

What TST PlotConnect offers

  • Verified plot and rental listings
  • Location filters by country, county, and area
  • Map-based browsing with pinned locations
  • Secure contact access after activation

Why people use us

  • Reduce the risk of fake listings
  • Save time with clear, searchable locations
  • Compare prices and listing details quickly
  • Talk directly to caretakers and owners

Our focus

We prioritize verified data, responsive support, and a smooth mobile-first experience. TST PlotConnect is built for students, families, and investors who want trusted property options.
