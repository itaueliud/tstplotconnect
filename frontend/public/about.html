About TST PlotConnect

TST PlotConnect helps you discover verified land and rental listings across Kenya. We focus on transparency, location clarity, and contact access so buyers and renters can make faster decisions.

Search by county and area, review listing details, and view locations on the map. Our goal is to simplify the process of finding trusted plots and rentals.

What we offer

  • Verified plot and rental listings
  • Location filters by country, county, and area
  • Map-based browsing
  • Secure contact access
