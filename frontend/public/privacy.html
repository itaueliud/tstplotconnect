Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TST PlotConnect collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, mobile app, and related services.

Information we collect

  • Name, phone number, country, and account profile details you provide during registration.
  • Login and account activity needed to keep your account secure and signed in.
  • Listing search filters and in-app activity used to improve search and support experiences.
  • Payment-related records such as activation status, payment history, transaction references, and timestamps.
  • Support messages or communications sent through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, or Instagram.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To help you discover accommodation listings and unlock listing contact details after activation.
  • To process payments, verify access status, and maintain payment logs.
  • To respond to support requests and improve service reliability.
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, or misuse of the platform.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only when needed to operate the service, including with payment providers, hosting providers, analytics or infrastructure services, and where required by law.

Contact details for accommodation listings may be shown to you only after your access is activated according to the platform rules.

Data retention and security

We keep data for as long as it is reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce platform policies.

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

  • You may contact us to update or correct your account information.
  • You may request help with account access or account-related concerns through our support channels.
  • You may stop using the service at any time, though some records may be retained where legally or operationally required.

Children's privacy

TST PlotConnect is not intended for children. If you believe personal information from a child has been provided to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.
