## Full description

PlotConnect helps you discover verified accommodation faster.

Browse hostels, bedsitters, and lodges in one mobile app, compare prices, narrow results by country, county, area, category, and budget, and open listing details when you find a space that fits your needs.

Key features:

- Search accommodation by country, county, area, category, and price range
- Browse verified listings with pricing and location details
- View listing details before deciding to activate access
- Unlock caretaker phone and WhatsApp contact details with one-tap activation
- Manage your account, payment activity, and access status in one place
- Get support by email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram

PlotConnect is designed to make accommodation discovery simpler, faster, and more trustworthy for users looking for hostels, bedsitters, and short-stay options.
